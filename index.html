1. Introduction

Welcome to Weft ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile and desktop application Weft (the "App").

By using Weft, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Notes and Content: Text, drawings, images, and files you create within the app are stored locally on your device.
  • Google Account (Optional): If you choose to use the sync feature, we access your Google account email for authentication purposes only.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT collect usage analytics
  • We do NOT sell any data to third parties

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage

All your notes, drawings, and files are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

3.2 Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable the "Freedom Sync" feature:

  • Your data is synced to your own Google Drive account
  • Data is stored in a folder named "Weft" in your Google Drive
  • We do NOT have access to your Google Drive or synced data
  • You can delete this data at any time from your Google Drive

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In for authentication when you choose to sync your data. When you sign in with Google:

  • We receive your email address and display name
  • We request access to Google Drive to save your backup files
  • This information is used solely for the sync feature

Google's privacy policy applies to their services: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All communication with Google services uses HTTPS encryption
  • Your data is stored on your device and your personal Google Drive
  • We do not transmit your data to our servers

6. Children's Privacy

Weft does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our app is designed for general audiences and does not contain any age-restricted content.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible on your device
  • Delete: You can delete the app and all local data at any time
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke Google Drive access in your Google Account settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
